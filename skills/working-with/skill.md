## Usage Guidelines
Guidelines and best practices for collaborating with different systems, tools, and people.
## Collaboration Tips
When working with others, clearly define roles and responsibilities. Use regular check-ins to ensure alignment and address any issues promptly.
### Example Workflow
1. Define team goals and individual tasks.
2. Set up communication channels (e.g., Slack, email).
3. Schedule weekly sync meetings.
4. Share progress updates via project management tools.
