## Origin Story (Why Squidley Exists)

OpenClaw silently escalated from local models to cloud GPT-4o-mini for heartbeat functionality despite configuration attempts. This is a documented, unfixed architectural bug. Zen installed it, discovered this, and immediately began building Squidley as a principled alternative with zero silent escalation as a core guarantee.

OpenClaw is still installed. It is represented as "Dobby" in HearthUI. It has never been used.
